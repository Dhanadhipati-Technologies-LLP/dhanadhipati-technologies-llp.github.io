What Is Ludo Wordo?

Ludo Wordo is an innovative, strategy-based board game where players collect alphabets, move pawns intelligently, and complete a target word to win the match.

  • It is not about racing or memorization, but about strategy and smart positioning
  • Every move allows players to collect alphabets placed across the Ludo path
  • Opponents’ alphabets influence your strategy
  • The first player to collect the correct alphabets and complete the word wins

The Core Objective

  • One target word is shown at the start of each match
  • Some alphabets of the word are missing
  • Players must identify and collect the missing alphabets
  • The first player to complete the word wins instantly

How the Gameplay Works

Pawns and Movement

  • Players move pawns based on the number shown on dice rolls
  • The Ludo game path is filled with alphabets
  • There is no finish point — pawns keep moving until the word is completed

Collecting Alphabets

  • Landing on each square cell on the Ludo path allows the player to collect that alphabet
  • Collected alphabets are stored in a dedicated Alphabet Collection page

Viewing Your Alphabet Collection

Tap the big center button on the Ludo board (the home area in classic Ludo) to open the Alphabet Collection page.

  • Top section shows the target word with missing alphabets
  • Middle section shows your collected alphabets
  • Bottom section shows opponents’ collected alphabets
  • Use this page anytime to plan strategy and target opponents

Drag and Place System

  • Drag collected alphabets to the correct missing position in the word
  • If correct, it locks into place and updates for all players
  • If incorrect, it returns back to the collection set

Pawn Battles

  • Killing an opponent’s pawn allows you to collect one alphabet from them
  • Targeted attacks can directly change the outcome of the match

Clues

When unable to guess the word, clues are available.

  • Meaning-based clues
  • Pronunciation-based clues

Multiplayer & Game Modes

  • Room-based invitations
  • 2-player and 4-player matches

Magic Boxes

  • Provide surprise gifts

7 Words a Week – Root-Based Learning

Every week, players receive 7 connected words built around one root word.

  • Day 1 introduces the root word and the first weekly word
  • Each of the next 6 days reveals one new word
  • All 7 words are connected to the same root
  • Each word includes meaning, memory trick, example, part of speech, synonyms, antonyms, and grammar rules
  • Understanding the root helps remember all 7 words easily
