1. Introduction

Ludo Wordo ("the Game") is developed and operated by Dhanadhipati Technologies LLP, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India.

We are committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: User-provided names and profile pictures used for Leaderboards.
  • Identifiers: Unique Device IDs (Android ID) and Advertising IDs (AAID) for analytics and game functionality.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (City/Country level) for regional Leaderboards. We do not collect precise GPS location.
  • Usage Data: In-game activity such as levels completed, words found, and coins earned.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party service providers to enable features and advertisements within the Game. These providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services & Firebase (Analytics & Leaderboards)
  • Google AdMob (Advertisements)
  • IronSource (Advertisements)
  • Unity Ads (Advertisements)

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain user data as long as the account remains active. Users may request permanent data deletion by emailing: cs.dhanadhipatitechnologies@gmail.com

All verified deletion requests will be processed within 7 business days.

5. Children’s Privacy

Ludo Wordo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
